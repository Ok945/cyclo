Our theme

Pedal For Life, Road Safety Drive

"Cyclothon - Pedal for life, Road Safety Drive", our vision is simple yet profound: to create a safer tomorrow for all by empowering communities with the knowledge and tools to champion road safety. We envision a world where every road user, from cyclists to pedestrians, motorists to public transport users, can travel without fear and with confidence in their safety. Through our thrilling Cyclothon, ZEST'24 aims to ignite awareness about road safety and responsible road practices.

Route
